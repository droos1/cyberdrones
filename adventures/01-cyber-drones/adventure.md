# Adventure 1: Cyber Drones

## Background

**Arasaka** has developed a new form of highly illegal "droneware" - cyberware that allows taking over a person and running them via remote control. Includes full memory wipe.

A rogue corp executive (**Lazard**) was running the program under false authorization (listed as "focus boosters and fear suppressing cyberware"). It's getting out of control. He plans to eliminate the scientists, ship the tech offshore, and deploy in dark ops in third world conflicts.

## Major NPCs

| NPC | Role | Details |
|-----|------|---------|
| **Ryla Vox** "The Media" | Independent journalist, News 54 | Got anonymous tip + doctored video of the shooting |
| **The Mole** | Arasaka insider scientist | Principled, has netrunner skills. Very monitored comms. Helping players covertly |
| **Lazard** "The Corp" | Droneware project sponsor | Plans to kill scientists, move tech offshore, deploy in dark ops |
| **Dr. Devon Shard** "The Scientist" | Operates control deck for Lazard | Has the key card, access to facility |

## Recent Backstory
- Arasaka develops droneware
- Knowledge spreading too wide, so Corp staged an "accident evacuation" and used test subjects (the PCs) to exterminate the scientists
- Plan: have players caught by police. Redacted footage leaked to media to frame them.

## Adventure Structure

**Time pressure: 8 hours from waking up to disabling the control signal**

---

### Part 1: Wake Up

Players wake up in a safe house where they were "stored" pending further missions.

**The Apartment:**
- Mattresses on floor, folding tables, monitors, TV
- TV running News 54 (sound off)
- CCTV monitors showing area around house
- Door: high-tech keylock, reinforced
- Box of grenades
- Kibble food packs

**News Footage (Ryla Vox):**
- Shows people in an alley, players surround them
- Victims holding up hands, begging
- Players open fire
- Trauma team response - "massacre of innocent workers outside Arasaka facility"
- Police + Arasaka statement: "cyberpsychotic mercenary gang"
- Footage is grainy with glitches. AWARENESS/TECH check: looks doctored

**The Mole calls (disguised voice):**
- "Their netrunners are tracing signals"
- "I've scrambled the codes, buys you max 8 hours"
- "I'll help you but you need to help me"
- "Wet ops team incoming - GET OUT"
- Can't reveal identity (they could extract it from PCs)
- Provides 2 contact codes

**Egress:**
- Sniper shot through window (5d6, window absorbs 5)
- CCTV shows tac team at front door
- AWARENESS: claymore on rear exit
- Fire escape exists but sniper covers it (INT check to realize)

**Enemy Forces:**
- 2 vans, 4 techs with stretchers (Pistols 2d6)
- 3 wet ops: Base 14, HP 45, Armor 9, Heavy SMG 3d6
- Boss: Base 16, HP 50, Armor 11, Heavy SMG 3d6
- Reinforcements: another van + AV-4 with door gunner

**Exit Options:** Grab a van / Get a cab / Run and mingle (evasion)

---

### Part 2: Investigation

**Safe Locations** (from character backgrounds):
- Oxide Bar - no cops, useful contacts
- Tech buddy's storage shed - longer trip, gear available
- Girlfriend's apartment - safest, no connections

**Finding the Media - Pulse Point Cafe:**
- Full of media people, PCs will be recognized
- Need stealth/disguise to approach
- **Ryla's story:**
  - Anonymous tip + pre-cut video (clearly doctored but ran it anyway)
  - Had a netrunner trace it - points to Arasaka origin
  - Netrunner has gone silent (can't reach them)

**Drone Encounter:**
- Two drones attack (civilians under control)
- Booster/biker crashes bike into players
- Base 14 (reduced from drone effect), Shotgun 5d6
- AWARENESS: not normal, zoned out, pain resistant
- AWARENESS (hard): same bulge as players have
- Fight to the death, first drone encounter

**Getting the Footage (3 cameras):**

1. **Arasaka Cam** - Grey wall, data ports. Insane ICE, basically impenetrable
2. **Garage Cam** - Pathetic security, already hacked. Bad angle footage: people coming out, commotion, back of someone in alley
3. **Pachinko Cam** - Gambling operation, internal network. Must physically go in and connect. Penetrate 3 data walls while playing pachinko

**Pachinko Table (1000 EB per 100 balls):**

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Drug buzz (-50 balls, twice = addicted) |
| 2 | +1000 EB |
| 3 | Electroshock 1 dmg (-100 balls) |
| 4 | Open vault (double effect next round) |
| 5 | -50 balls |
| 6 | -100 balls |
| 7 | +50 balls |
| 8 | Open vault |
| 9 | Pleasure wave (lose 1 turn) |
| 10 | Jackpot +5000 EB |

**Pachinko Guard - Rhino:** REF 8, Kevlar coat SP 18, scanning optics, CAWS shotgun

**The Key Footage (from Pachinko cam):**
- Strobe on door starts up
- People stumble out - two wearing goggles, one has bloody surgeon gloves
- They look shaken, confused, gesture back at building
- Players walk in from alleyways (PCs recognize themselves)
- AWARENESS: movement is wrong - jilted, too-still when standing
- A corp steps out behind the players, another corp/scientist holds a small cyberdeck
- Quick count of victims, one recognizes him and approaches
- Card inserted in deck, hits a button, turns and walks away
- Players blast the scientists
- Street muscle (Rhino) walks through bodies, looting. Dying victim grabs him, talks to him

**Rhino (if talked to):**
- Can't be intimidated, can be bribed
- Recorded everything
- "Lazard and his fucking Devon Shard are fucking us"
- "Get help, it's all on level 17"
- Wants no part of it

**Identifying Victims (5 persons):**
- Researcher in bioware, memory augment, skill chipping
- Recruited from Biotech labs
- Military signals and encryptions specialist
- All held in total seclusion in Arasaka
- All work on level 17, direct elevator, restricted access

**By now players should know:**
- Control center is in Arasaka building, level 17
- They need to extract the mole
- Corp called Lazard is responsible
- His tech guy (Devon Shard) has the key card
- It's a black ops program

---

### Part 3: Grab the Access Card

Devon Shard moving from clinic to apartment complex in 30 min. Currently at a high-end outdoor cafe on a date.

**The Grab:**
- Drive-by snatch, toss into van
- Two heavily chipped guards present
- Choice: just grab card, or bring him

**If grabbed:**
- "I'm fucking wired, assholes"
- He shuts down - warning voice: "Subject has been shut down until rescue. Cooperate and..."
- Option: cut out his receiver and stash him as evidence (advanced medtech)

**Guards:**
- Cyber optics, Dermal armor SP 14, HP 55
- Very Heavy Pistols 4d6 AP

Arasaka gets tipped off - must move fast.

---

### Part 4: Shut Down the Signal

Get into the Arasaka lab (level 17), destroy the control keys to permanently lock Arasaka out of the players' brains.

**Opposition waves:**

| Type | SP | HP | Weapon |
|------|----|----|--------|
| Accounting cyber drones | 0 | 35 | Heavy Pistol 3d6 |
| Regular corp guards | 9 | 40 | Heavy SMG 4d6 |
| Control room sec ops | - | - | Lasers 5d6, Base 15 |
| Heavy tactical team | 18 | - | Fully cybered (arrives after a few rounds) |

**Environment:**
- Window: SP 80 (massive)
- Can trigger fire suppression: visibility 10m, all actions -5

---

### Part 5: Epilogue - Coming in from the Cold

With signal shutdown, players choose:

1. **Go to authorities** - Arasaka takes reputational damage. Lazard arrested but lawyers get him out on technicality
2. **Barter with rival corp (Militech)** - Trade tech for safety. "The genie is out of the bottle"
3. **Sell souls to Arasaka** - Offer silence for safety and inside jobs. But they'll never know if one night they won't wake up again

## Events (Random/Triggered)

- **Signal breakthrough:** A player goes back under control. -5 on all actions, tries to kill the others.
